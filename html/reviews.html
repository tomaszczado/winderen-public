Alicia
My horse was a head shaker, puller, behind the bit, he even reared once. I did it all… I had an equine dentist come out, she told me the bit I was using fit him well and did not touch any teeth or pinch. I had a equine chiropractor come out, and they told me he was in prime condition with the issues to cause any pain while riding. So, what was it?!! I guess he just didn’t like metal in his mouth. I bought a cheap rubber bit from the local tack shop and BAM. He loved it. But, it was torn up after 2 rides. I then stepped up to the happy mouth, and within a week, there were sharp edges. When I found Winderen products I noticed a year warranty. Even if the bit lasted 6 months, it would pay for itself and last WAYYY longer than any other alternative to metal bit. The price was worth it, and my horse LOVES IT!
