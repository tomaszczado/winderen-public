Join winderen
Partnership Program

Winderen was created with passion for horse riding and innovative technologies. As a result, our products gain new customers within riders of all skill levels every month. Now we open up for collaborations!
You can join people who trusted Winderen and decided to work with us.

We present our Partnership Program that enables you to enter the sales network, gather your own group of clients, and take the advantage of benefits available exclusively for active Partners

Joining the Partnership Program is free.
You gain every single time a product is bought via your referral.

Benefits of
Partnership Program

PERMANENT

Once a client is assigned to you, they remain your client. That lets you grow your network

TWO-LEVEL

Once your client decides to join the Partnership Program too, you gain additional benefits from their clients’ transactions. That enables you to create a network of clients and Partners.

ATTRACTIVE

Every order made by clients from your network gains you points you can exchange for discounts on our products.

TRANSPARENT

Joining the Partnership program is free of charge. You gain every single time a product is bought through your referral.

Who we ARE
looking for?

We are looking for people with a broad network in riding communities. People who treat riding both as a hobby but also as a professional job - riders, trainers, vets, physiotherapists, or osteopaths.

We are open to collaborate with influencers, bloggers, people active on social media, and competition organizers.

If you like the Winderen brand and you wish to see it in your country, contact us. We are interested in international market development as well!
