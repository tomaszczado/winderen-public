Gel Bits System

Horse-friendly gel mouthpieces
Durable against destruction from chewing
Interchangeable cheek pieces system

Achieve complete harmony with your horse, maintaining
light, stable and precise contact with its mouth

Winderen Gel Bits System bits are made of durable synthetic gel that’s comfortable and readily accepted by horses, and at the same time is extremely resistant to being destroyed by chewing.

The Gel Bits System is also an innovative approach for riders who use more than one bit in their daily training. Each of our gel mouthpieces can be combined with different types of cheek pieces in order to create the right bit for you. The whole process of changing cheek pieces is very simple and takes just a few moments.

especially for

Horses that have problems with getting on the bit and yielding to the hand

Sensitive horses that have difficulty accepting a metal mouthpiece

Horses allergic to metal bits

Horse-friendly bits

For over three years we have been running in-depth tests and observations which ultimately led us to develop our Gel Bits System bits. During this period, we tested our ideas on hundreds of horses and riders with varying levels of skill and experience.

This extensive group, consisting of professional riders, amateurs and leisure riders, helped lead us to the right conclusions. As a result, we’ve created a gel that our test horses easily accepted and that is extremely durable against destruction from horses’ teeth. Many of our test riders observed that their horses were chewing the bit more willingly and relaxing faster during work without evading the bit. The horses were getting on the bit quickly and working with a good, stable contact, which made training sessions more effective and relaxed.

Safe for horses

The synthetic gel used in a Winderen mouthpiece has been fully tested and found to be completely safe for horses. It has FDA certification, which is internationally recognised as proof of high standards, quality, and safety. This means that your horse can safely chew the Winderen bit with no ill effects

Durable against destruction from
chewing

A big challenge in the design phase was to make a mouthpiece that’s both soft and comfortable for horses and strong enough to hold up to chewing. After many attempts, we finally managed to achieve it. The soft, gel structure is willingly chewed and accepted by horses and at the same time is extremely resistant to being destroyed by chewing.

We did a lot of tests with horses, which, to the despair of their owners, destroyed different synthetic mouthpieces very quickly. These same horses didn’t destroy the Winderen gel bit. We even let dogs chew on it to ensure the strength of our gel. As carnivores, they have much sharper teeth and stronger jaws. Winderen bits performed more than satisfactorily*

* We assure you that no dogs were harmed during
the tests – on the contrary, they had quite a good time

Gel bits system

Horse-friendy gel bits with interechangeable
cheek pieces system.

As we all know, choosing the right bit can be quite a challenge. A horse’s needs can change over time, so when designing the Winderen Gel Bits System we wanted to make the process of selecting the right bit for the needs and temperament of your horse as easy as possible.

This is why we have created a system of interchangeable cheek pieces and mouthpieces. This innovative solution lets you connect any of our mouthpieces with our entire range of cheek pieces, allowing a wide range of possibilities and modifications in the functionality of our bits. The other important thing is that mouthpieces and cheek pieces can be quickly and independently interchanged as often as you like.

Compliance with
FEI requirements

One of the priorities we set when designing the Gel Bits System bits was its compliance with FEI requirements. Thanks to that we were able to design a bit that meets guidelines of the jumping, dressage and eventing rules:

Horses that have problems with getting on the bit and yielding to the hand
Sensitive horses that have difficulty accepting a metal mouthpiece
Horses that destroy synthetic bits by chewing on them
Smooth surface of the bit
Made of synthetic, durable material
All mouthpieces’ hardness allowed by the rules
Tongue relief port with acceptable height
18 mm thickness, suitable for both horses and ponies
cheekpieces allowed for dressage (loose ring, eggbutt, D-ring and baucher)
the shape of the curb bit and all lengths of cheekpieces allowed by the rules
white and orange bit color not forbidden in all disciplines
Our Gel Bits System complies with the 25th edition of the FEI Dressage Rules, the 26th edition of the FEI Jumping Rules and the 25th edition of the FEI Eventing Rules.

The unwanted reaction that often accompanies youngest and delicate horses during their first steps under the saddle and the „cold back” effect after fastening the girth were both completely eliminated which is a clear signal to me that I provide my horses with comfort and freedom. This is definitely my number one on the market.”

„I had the opportunity to participate in the testing of the Winderen girth prototypes. I have put them on many horses and have noticed some really positive traits of this product. The Winderen girth is horse-friendly and gives them freedom of movement. Thanks to the wide sternum part, it definitely stabilizes the saddle and distributes the pressure better, which results in better comfort for the horse. I feel that the girth improves their well-being during training, which translates into joy from work :) And we all want to choose the equipment so as to increase comfort giving the horses what is best :)”
Katarzyna Łukasik

The system of fastenings on different levels is also very practical for me. Not only because the buckles don’t push my legs away, but also because this system also allows me to fasten the girth to the back billet first, which is a saddling method recommended by many physiotherapists.”
